JobInsight — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

What data we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address only. This is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. It is not shared with third parties.

How we use your data

  • Email address — Used to identify you within the extension and for rate limiting on our backend server.
  • Job description text — When you view a LinkedIn job listing, the job description text is sent to our backend server for AI analysis. It is not stored after analysis is complete.

Third-party services

  • Google OAuth — Used for sign-in. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI API — Job descriptions are sent to OpenAI for analysis. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Data storage

Your email and cached job analysis results are stored locally in your browser. No personal data is stored on our servers.

Data deletion

Click "Sign out" in the extension popup to remove your data. Uninstalling the extension removes all local data.
